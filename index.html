About Me

Jenny Kam grew up in Brooklyn, New York. She had interests in STEM from an early age, studying at Stuyvesant High School and going on to graduate from Boston University with a degree in Biomedical Engineering. She grew to love coding in her studies, continuing to complete a bootcamp at the Flatiron School, and now uses technology to connect to and work on projects she is passionate about.
